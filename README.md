## üìö Modules & Topics

The application is structured into chronological and thematic sections:

### Historical & Theoretical Foundations
*   **Ancient Philosophy**: The roots of Western thought (Socrates, Plato, Aristotle).
*   **Modern Philosophy**: The shift to rationalism and empiricism.
*   **Contemporary Philosophy**: 19th & 20th-century developments.
*   **Metaphysical**: Inquiries into the nature of reality.
*   **Epistemological**: The study of knowledge and belief.
*   **Logical**: Principles of valid reasoning.
*   **Ethical**: Frameworks for moral judgement.

### Applied Ethics & Global Issues
*   **World Poverty**: Economic justice and global responsibility.
*   **Abortion**: Moral status of the fetus and rights of the mother.
*   **Euthanasia**: The right to die, autonomy, and the sanctity of life.

### Technology & Society
*   **IT & Quality of Life**: Impact of technology on human well-being.
*   **Internet & Democracy**: The digital age's influence on political systems.
*   **Internet Crimes**: Cyber ethics, privacy, and digital rights.
